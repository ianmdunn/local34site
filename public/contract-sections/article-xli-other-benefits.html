ARTICLE XLI

Other Benefits

The University will offer the following benefits to Staff Members to the extent they are offered to University Managerial and Professional employees, subject to eligibility and other provisions of each program. 1. Scholarship Program for Sons and Daughters which shall include full-time attendance at a Community College. 2. Adoption Reimbursement 3. Same-sex spouse, civil union partnership and domestic partnership tax credit. 4. Sex Reassignment Surgery.
