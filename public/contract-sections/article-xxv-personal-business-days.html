Article XXV — Personal Business Days

ARTICLE XXV

Personal Business Days

1. Each non-probationary Staff Member employed prior to July 1, 2009 may take personal time and receive pay for straight-time hours not worked while on such personal time for up to four (4) days of absence during each University fiscal year. 2. A Staff Member may use personal time in hours or days. 3. A Staff Member must give his or her Supervisor as much advance notice of the desired personal time as is practicable. Requests for use of personal business days shall not be denied arbitrarily or capriciously. 4. A day, for purposes of this Article, is the number of hours determined by dividing the Staff Member’s scheduled hours of straight-time work in a normal two (2) week period by ten (10). 5. Personal time may not be used to extend a vacation, except by mutual agreement of the Staff Member and the Staff Member’s Supervisor.
