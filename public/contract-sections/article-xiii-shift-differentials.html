Article XIII — Shift Differentials

ARTICLE XIII

Shift Differentials

1. The work units in which Staff Members work have various arrangements with regard to shift differentials and other premiums for working at various times or on various schedules. Each work unit shall maintain such shift differentials and other premiums at no less than the level in effect on April 3, 1984, and in no event less than five percent (5%) of a Staff Member’s hourly rate per hour of work. 2. The hours to be considered the day, evening and night shifts for purposes of application of this Agreement in any work unit which operates on a three (3) shift basis shall be the same as at present.
